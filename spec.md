## Implementation Plan

### Backend
- Minimal Motoko backend (greeting actor only) - no database or admin panel needed

### Frontend
- Design theme: orange, yellow, and black color palette
- Font: Cabinet Grotesk for headings, General Sans for body
- Navigation: Fixed top nav with links to all sections, hamburger menu for mobile
- Hero/Banner: Full-width hero with shop name in Hindi, tagline, CTA button
- Featured Products: Grid of product cards with placeholder images, names, prices, descriptions
- Products Section: Full product catalog grid - shoes, slippers, sandals, boots, loafers
- New Offers Section: Offer cards with discount badges and promotional content
- Collections Section: Trending/new collection showcase with visual cards
- About Section: Owner info (Ayush Saha), business story, shop values
- Contact Section: Address, phone, email details + frontend-only contact form
- Footer: Logo, links, social icons, copyright
- All sections use smooth scroll behavior
- Fully responsive with mobile-first approach
